Sex and relationships therapist

A specialist who helps individual clients and couples cope with sexual and emotional issues in their relationships. Their work is focused on improving the quality of intimate life and creating healthy, trusting relationships. Such a professional possesses knowledge in psychology, sexual health, communication, and often works with issues related to sexual dysfunction, sexual orientation, intimacy, as well as emotional and psychological problems in relationships. A sex therapist and relationship consultant may use various methods, including discussions, exercises, and meditations, to enhance the emotional and physical connection in relationships. They help with both individual issues and matters affecting the couple or polyamorous union, guiding them towards better understanding and harmony in their relationships.
