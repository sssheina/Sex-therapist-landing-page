Psychodemia

An educational institution dedicated to training experts in the field of psychology and psychotherapy. It offers a wide range of programs and courses designed to provide students with theoretical knowledge and practical skills in various psychological disciplines. Psychodemia emphasizes a comprehensive understanding of human behavior, therapeutic techniques, and the application of psychological principles in real-world settings. The institution is licensed, confirming its right to provide educational activities in psychology and psychotherapy. Established in 2016, it is based in Moscow.
